⚗️ CHEM PARTY

The Chemistry Mini-Game Board Game

How to Play

Roll the dice and move around the board. Collect Atoms (⚛) and buy Mols (◎) to win!

Blue Spaces: +3 Atoms

Red Spaces: -3 Atoms

Mini-Game Spaces: Compete in chemistry trivia for +5 Atoms!

Event Spaces: Draw a random card (good or bad!)

When you are near the Mol Marker (◎), you can buy a Mol for 10 Atoms. Mols are worth 10 Atoms at the end of the game.
